Chester Bennington

Lead singer of Linkin Park. with Mike Shinoda.

I came across Chester Bennington when I came across his unique singing style in a band called Linking Park back in my 20's. He could sing both like an angel and with rage, which is very hard to do and still have a voice long term. I have loved hearing his songs ever since.

It has been 6 years since the report of his death, and he still being mourned by those who knew him. his fans and family.

He had a rough snildhood and earlt teens that shaped him into the adult we knew. The only thing he every wanted to do was write songs. Alot of his (their) songs invlove feelings and topics that everyone can relate to.


Linkin Park Albums


  1. Hybrid Theory Album Released in October 2000, and was interpretations of universal feelings, emotions, and experiences that everyone have or feel.


  2. Meteora Released in March 2003, was all about tthem experimenting. They did not care what others were doing and went with the feel of it. Each song is unique. This was started when they were on tour with Hybrid Theory and Reanimation (remix album in July 2002).


  3. Minute to Midnight Released in May 2007, and marked a beginning of deviation from their signature nu metal sound. It was delayed several times for several reasons, including side projects that Chester and Mike Shinoda were making as well as others.


  4. A Thousand Suns Released in September 2010, and was all about trying to deal with human fears, such as nuclear warfare. Was a deperture from what they had already released, carrying on their experimentation.


  5. Living Things Released in June 2012 and was about finding themselves again as they looked again at their music. This was to create a new sound whilst also getting back to their roots.


  6. The Hunting Party Album Released in June 2014, and was a return to their origins of nu rock. Albums befor and since Hybrid Theory had become heavily influence by electronic vibes.


  7. One More Light Album Released in May 2017, and was one of their last albums if not the last one before he died. This one is more of a pop sound and a departue from rock and electronic.
